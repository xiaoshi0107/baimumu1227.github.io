Marriage Bless

发表于 2021-07-19 阅读次数：

​ Hello everyone! It’s my great honor to attend my best friend’s wedding. They have been in love for 6 years and now they decide to become more than lovers but a family.

​ You both are the most generous and brilliant people I have met and I feel so happy for them to be a family in law. What I want to say is that the celebration is not the end but a beginning, the start of a new process. And I’m sure that all will go well in your life together.

​ With all my heart I wish you both the best of luck and increasing happiness as the years goes by. May peace, health and bright future attend both your bride and yourself until the end.
